Brown Butter Cookies

These cookies are some of the best i've come across. Browning the butter gives it a rich, nutty flavor. The high ratio of brown sugar alongside the yogurt ensures they stay moist

Just make sure you add all the wet ingredients at the right time- i forgot to add the yogurt until after the dough was formed last time and they didn't come out right

Ingredients

  • 2.25 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • .5 tsp salt
  • 2 stick butter
  • 1.5 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 yolk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp greek yogurt
  • 1.5 cup chocolate chips
  1. Brown the Butter
  2. mix butter and sugars. Then add rest of wet ingredients
  3. Slowly add dry stuff to wet, mix until combined
  4. chill the dough
  5. bake ~10 mins
