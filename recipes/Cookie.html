Ingredients

  • 2.25 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • .5 tsp salt
  • 2 stick butter
  • 1.5 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 yolk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp greek yogurt
  • 1.5 cup chocolate chips
  1. Brown the Butter
  2. mix butter and sugars. Then add rest of wet ingredients
  3. Slowly add dry stuff to wet, mix until combined
  4. chill the dough
  5. bake ~10 mins
